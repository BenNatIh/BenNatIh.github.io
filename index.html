Robotics

Aerial Installation of Bird-Safety Dot Patterns

Ongoing lab project (Flight Systems and Control Lab, UTIAS): an aerial manipulator, an OpenMANIPULATOR-X arm mounted on a Holybro X650 quadcopter, that autonomously applies bird-safe window markers to meet FLAP Canada spacing guidelines. Designed a reel-based marker feed mechanism to minimize payload, and implemented a whole-body SE(3) geometric feedback-linearization controller coupling quadrotor and 4-DOF arm dynamics, validated in NVIDIA Isaac Sim with an automated gain-sweep harness for systematic tuning.

Robotics + RL

Robotic Arm Control with RL

Group project benchmarking TD3, SAC, and PPO against a PID baseline on the FetchReach robotic arm reaching task, across dense/sparse rewards and noisy observations. My contribution: built the PPO-Clip actor-critic agent and a custom on-policy variant of Hindsight Experience Replay (HER is normally off-policy only) to make sparse rewards tractable, and ran the dense/sparse and noise experiments benchmarking it against the team's PID, TD3, and SAC results.

Intent Prediction for Human-Robot Interaction

Group project fusing pose and gaze to predict whether a person intends to interact with a robot, trained across four combined public datasets. My contribution: combined the gaze datasets, and built the gaze prediction MLP and the temporal transformer used for pose-based intent classification, matching a published reference model trained on ~10x less data.
